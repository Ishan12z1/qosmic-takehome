**Brew Mindfully, Sip Calmly**

**Zen Rojas Tea Infuser** is designed to make your **daily ritual cleaner**, **easier,** and more **enjoyable**. Whether you’re new to loose-leaf tea or transitioning from store-bought tea bags, this infuser **removes the guesswork and mess**, allowing your blends to fully expand and steep **for richer flavor.** Crafted for **convenience** and **reusability,** it **helps you enjoy every cup** the way tea is meant to be experienced—fresh, full-bodied, and intentional. **Perfect for beginners** and **tea lovers** alike, it **brings simplicity** back to your brewing routine while **preserving the quality** of every blend.

**Why You'll Love It**

* Quick and convenient brewing
* Easy cleanup after each use
* Reusable and eco-friendly
* Durable, heat-resistant material
* Universal cup and mug fit

**How to Use**

* Fill tea infuser with **2g** of loose-leaf tea
* Steep in **8oz**hot water for **5-7** minutes

* Remove infuser and **enjoy** your perfect brew
